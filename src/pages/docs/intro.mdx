# Welcome to PizzaWorld Documentation

PizzaWorld is the ultimate pizza management dashboard designed to streamline your restaurant operations, boost sales, and deliver exceptional customer experiences.

## Quick Start Guide

### 1. Sign Up for PizzaWorld

1. Visit [pizzaworld.com](https://pizzaworld.com)
2. Click "Start Free Trial"
3. Fill out the registration form
4. Verify your email address

### 2. Set Up Your Restaurant Profile

Once you've created your account, you'll need to configure your restaurant settings:

- **Restaurant Information**: Name, address, phone number
- **Operating Hours**: Set your business hours
- **Menu Items**: Add your pizza offerings and prices
- **Staff Accounts**: Create accounts for your team members

### 3. Configure Your Dashboard

Customize your dashboard to show the metrics that matter most to your business:

- Sales analytics
- Order management
- Inventory tracking
- Staff performance
